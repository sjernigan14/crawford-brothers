Carter-Finley Stadium hosts more than just NC State Wolfpack football. Major concerts, conferences, and events draw thousands of guests to Raleigh's premier entertainment venue. Why Crawford Brothers for Your Carter-Finley Visit

Game days and event nights require timing. You need a restaurant that understands the rhythm of celebration—one that can turn a pre-game meal into an event unto itself. Crawford Brothers delivers exactly that. Our dry-aged prime steaks arrive seared to precision. Our craft cocktails come from a bar run by Beverage Director Jordan Joseph. Our wine list spans 300+ bottles, sourced for depth and accessibility.

The kitchen isn't hidden. Floor-to-ceiling glass walls showcase our dry-aging program in real time. You'll see the cuts aging, understand the method, taste the difference on the plate. This transparency defines our approach—no mystery, no apology, just excellent steaks.

From Kickoff to the Final Out: Your Timeline

Pre-game: Arrive at 5pm. Order a cocktail. Study the menu. Let the steak speak for itself. You'll have time to linger without rushing toward the stadium.

Group celebrations: Our Private Dining Room seats up to 24. Need something larger? We offer full restaurant buyouts up to 70 people for tailgate-to-table events and group celebrations. Reserve through Tock and let us handle the details.

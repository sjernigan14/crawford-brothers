Private Dining & Events

Crawford Brothers offers three distinct private dining experiences, each led by the culinary team of James Beard-nominated Chef Scott Crawford. Whether you are planning a corporate dinner, rehearsal dinner, milestone celebration, or an intimate evening with friends, our team will create a custom event tailored to your group.

The Private Dining Room

Our Private Dining Room accommodates up to 24 guests in an elegant, semi-private space within the restaurant. The room features a dedicated service team and can be configured for seated dinners, cocktail receptions, or a combination of both. Choose from a Family Style Dinner (3, 4, or 5 courses) or a Plated Dinner (3 or 4 courses). Sample menus feature dishes like Fresh Oysters with Traditional Accompaniments, Poached King Crab with Caviar Beurre Blanc, Seared Diver Scallops, Braised Beef Short Rib with Robuchon Potatoes and Barolo Glaze, Prime Ribeye, Australian Lamb Chop, and Baked Alaska. A la carte additions include Custom Cake, Audiovisual, and Flowers. A final guest count is required seven days before the event.

The Gangster Room

The Gangster Room is unlike any private dining experience in the Triangle. This exclusive table seats 6 guests behind the glass wall of our in-house dry aging room. Dine from 5 to 11pm with a direct view of prime cuts aging on the rack. Order from the regular dinner menu, or book the Butcher's Feast for the Table — a showpiece multi-course meal featuring a Seafood Tower, Tableside Steak Tartare, Crab Cake, Roasted Bone Marrow, Japanese A5 Strip Loin, Snake River Farms Eye of the Rib, Prime New York Strip, Dry-Aged Bone-In Ribeye, and Tableside Baked Alaska.

Exclusive Gangster Room beverage experiences include Tableside Martini Service, Tableside Old Fashioned, a curated Whiskey Tasting, Sommelier Coursed Wine Pairing, and the Gangster Coursed Wine Pairing. All selections are curated by our Bar Manager and must be confirmed 7 days prior to reservation.

Full Restaurant Buyouts

Full Restaurant Buyouts accommodate up to 70 guests and give you exclusive use of the entire Crawford Brothers dining room, bar, and all private spaces. This is our most popular option for corporate holiday parties, rehearsal dinners, and milestone celebrations.

To inquire about private dining availability, pricing, and menus, please contact bryan@crawfordbrotherssteakhouse.com or call 919.629.4029. We recommend booking 2–4 weeks in advance for the Private Dining Room and Gangster Room, and 4–6 weeks for full restaurant buyouts.

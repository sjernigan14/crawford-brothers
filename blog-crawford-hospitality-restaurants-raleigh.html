Crawford Hospitality Restaurants in Raleigh & Cary

February 2026 Chef Scott Crawford

Discover the Crawford Hospitality Restaurant Family

Chef Scott Crawford has built something rare in the Raleigh-Cary dining scene: a restaurant group that celebrates quality, family, and singular culinary vision across five distinct concepts. Crawford Hospitality restaurants span from downtown Raleigh to Cary, offering everything from prime dry-aged steaks and French bistro fare to Adriatic seafood and intimate wine experiences.

Each restaurant stands on its own merit, but together they represent a philosophy: that great food is family business, and that the region deserves excellence at every level. Let's explore the Crawford Hospitality family.

Crawford Hospitality Group

Crawford & Son

The Original. Crawford & Son began it all—a neighborhood American restaurant on Person Street in downtown Raleigh that established Chef Scott Crawford's reputation for straightforward, ingredient-driven cooking. This is where the Crawford Hospitality story started.

Cuisine: Modern American

Location: Downtown Raleigh, Person Street

What makes it unique: The heart of the Crawford Hospitality family. Classic American dining with regional pride.

Jolie

French bistro in Historic Oakwood. Named for Chef Crawford's daughter, Jolie brings authentic French bistro dining to Raleigh's charming Historic Oakwood neighborhood. The rooftop dining experience and thoughtful wine program have earned recognition in the MICHELIN Guide. This is fine dining that feels like home.

Cuisine: French Bistro

Location: Historic Oakwood, Raleigh

What makes it unique: Rooftop dining, MICHELIN Guide recognized, family-named legacy.

Explore Jolie

Crawford Brothers Steakhouse

Prime steakhouse in Fenton, Cary. Located at 401 Fenton Gateway Drive, Crawford Brothers is the flagship steakhouse of the Crawford Hospitality restaurant group. Our in-house dry-aging program, 300+ bottle wine list, and private dining spaces make it the premier destination for business and special occasions in the Raleigh-Cary market.

Cuisine: Prime Steakhouse

Location: 401 Fenton Gateway Dr., Cary, NC 27511

What makes it unique: In-house dry-aging, 300+ wine list, private dining, open daily 5pm–10pm.

Visit Crawford Brothers

Brodeto

Adriatic Italian & Croatian seafood. At 2201 Iron Works Drive in Raleigh, Brodeto showcases Chef Crawford's passion for Mediterranean coastal cooking. The wood-fired grill, legendary brodeto stew, and traditional peka preparations bring the Adriatic to North Carolina. This is where Crawford Hospitality explores seafood with the same precision it brings to steaks.

Cuisine: Adriatic Italian & Croatian Seafood

Location: 2201 Iron Works Drive, Raleigh

What makes it unique: Wood-fired grill, brodeto stew, peka, Mediterranean authenticity.

Discover Brodeto

Bar Sousterre

Wine bar on Person Street, Raleigh. The newest addition to Crawford Hospitality, Bar Sousterre offers an intimate wine experience on downtown Raleigh's vibrant Person Street corridor. A natural complement to the broader Crawford Hospitality restaurant group.

Cuisine: Wine Bar

Location: Person Street, Raleigh

Why Choose Crawford Hospitality Restaurants?

When you dine at any Crawford Hospitality restaurant in Raleigh or Cary, you're experiencing the vision of a single chef—someone who understands that every plate, every wine list, every experience reflects the values of the entire group. Consistency, quality, family pride, and a commitment to the region.

Whether you're booking a business dinner at Crawford Brothers, celebrating an anniversary at Jolie, exploring seafood at Brodeto, or enjoying a glass of wine at Bar Sousterre, you're part of the Crawford Hospitality story.

Cocktails & Wine

Cocktails

Our cocktail program features original craft cocktails designed to complement our prime steaks and seafood. Experience classics reimagined for Crawford Brothers, including our signature Crawford Old Fashioned and Tableside Martini Service. Our bar team crafts each drink with precision and care, using premium spirits and fresh ingredients. Whether you prefer a timeless classic or one of our house creations, our cocktails are an essential part of the Crawford Brothers experience.

Ask about Tableside Old Fashioned preparation at your table for an interactive experience.

Wine

Our wine program is carefully curated to pair beautifully with our prime beef and fresh seafood. We work with our sommelier to build a collection that celebrates both classic regions and exciting discoveries. In the Gangster Room, we offer sommelier-led pairings designed specifically for our multi-course experiences.

Our wine selections range from bold reds that stand up to dry-aged prime cuts, to crisp whites that highlight our fresh seafood. We welcome wine enthusiasts and newcomers alike, and our team is always happy to offer recommendations.

After-Dinner

Complete your evening with our selection of amaro, fine spirits, and other after-dinner selections. Our bar manager has curated an exceptional collection of digestifs and fortified wines to end your dinner perfectly.

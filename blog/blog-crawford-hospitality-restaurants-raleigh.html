Discover the Crawford Hospitality Restaurant Family

Chef Scott Crawford has built something rare in the Raleigh-Cary dining scene: a restaurant group that celebrates quality, family, and singular culinary vision across five distinct concepts. Crawford Hospitality restaurants span from downtown Raleigh to Cary, offering everything from prime dry-aged steaks and French bistro fare to Adriatic seafood and intimate wine experiences.

Each restaurant stands on its own merit, but together they represent a philosophy: that great food is family business, and that the region deserves excellence at every level. Visit Crawford Hospitality to explore the full family.

Crawford & Son — The Original

Crawford & Son began it all—a neighborhood American restaurant on Person Street in downtown Raleigh that established Chef Scott Crawford's reputation for straightforward, ingredient-driven cooking. This is where the Crawford Hospitality story started. Modern American cuisine with regional pride.

Jolie — French Bistro in Historic Oakwood

Named for Chef Crawford's daughter, Jolie brings authentic French bistro dining to Raleigh's charming Historic Oakwood neighborhood. The rooftop dining experience and thoughtful wine program have earned recognition in the MICHELIN Guide. This is fine dining that feels like home. Explore Jolie.

Crawford Brothers Steakhouse — Prime Steakhouse in Fenton, Cary

Located at 401 Fenton Gateway Drive, Crawford Brothers is the flagship steakhouse of the Crawford Hospitality restaurant group. Our in-house dry-aging program, 300+ bottle wine list, and private dining spaces make it the premier destination for business and special occasions in the Raleigh-Cary market. Open daily 5pm–10pm. Visit Crawford Brothers.

Brodeto — Adriatic Italian & Croatian Seafood

At 2201 Iron Works Drive in Raleigh, Brodeto showcases Chef Crawford's passion for Mediterranean coastal cooking. The wood-fired grill, legendary brodeto stew, and traditional peka preparations bring the Adriatic to North Carolina. Discover Brodeto.

Bar Sousterre — Wine Bar on Person Street

The newest addition to Crawford Hospitality, Bar Sousterre offers an intimate wine experience on downtown Raleigh's vibrant Person Street corridor. A natural complement to the broader Crawford Hospitality restaurant group.

Why Choose Crawford Hospitality Restaurants?

When you dine at any Crawford Hospitality restaurant in Raleigh or Cary, you're experiencing the vision of a single chef—someone who understands that every plate, every wine list, every experience reflects the values of the entire group. Consistency, quality, family pride, and a commitment to the region.

Whether you're booking a business dinner at Crawford Brothers, celebrating an anniversary at Jolie, exploring seafood at Brodeto, or enjoying a glass of wine at Bar Sousterre, you're part of the Crawford Hospitality story.

What restaurants does Chef Scott Crawford own?

Chef Scott Crawford owns five restaurants in the Raleigh-Cary area under Crawford Hospitality: Crawford & Son, Jolie, Crawford Brothers Steakhouse, Brodeto, and Bar Sousterre.

Is Crawford Brothers related to Crawford & Son?

Yes. Both restaurants are owned by Chef Scott Crawford and operate under Crawford Hospitality. Crawford & Son was the original concept, while Crawford Brothers Steakhouse is the group's steakhouse in Cary.

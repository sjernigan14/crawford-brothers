The Gangster Room — Dining Behind the Glass

What is the Gangster Room?

An exclusive 6-seat table positioned behind the glass wall of our in-house dry-aging room. You dine surrounded by prime cuts aging on the rack, watching the program that makes our beef exceptional. The room is available nightly from 5 to 11pm. It is the only table of its kind in the Triangle.

What is the Butcher's Feast?

A multi-course experience designed to showcase everything we do: Seafood Tower, Tableside Steak Tartare, Crab Cake, Roasted Bone Marrow, Japanese A5 Strip Loin, Snake River Farms Eye of the Rib, Prime New York Strip, Dry-Aged Bone-In Ribeye, and Tableside Baked Alaska for dessert. The full journey takes about three hours and tells the story of our kitchen.

What drink experiences are available?

Tableside Martini Service, Tableside Old Fashioned, curated Whiskey Tasting, Sommelier Coursed Wine Pairing, and the Gangster Coursed Wine Pairing — all designed by our Bar Manager. All pairings are curated to complement the Butcher's Feast. Confirm your selections 7 days before your reservation so we have time to prepare.

How do I book the Gangster Room?

Reserve through Tock or contact bryan@crawfordbrotherssteakhouse.com. We recommend booking 2 to 4 weeks in advance, especially for weekends. The room is available for celebrations, corporate dinners, proposals, or any night you want something exceptional and memorable.

Can I order from the regular menu instead?

Yes. The Gangster Room has full access to our dinner menu. The Butcher's Feast is optional. Some guests prefer to order à la carte and simply enjoy the atmosphere of dining behind the glass, watching the steaks age while they eat.

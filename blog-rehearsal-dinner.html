Planning a Rehearsal Dinner in Raleigh & Cary

Can Crawford Brothers host a rehearsal dinner?

Yes. We host rehearsal dinners regularly — from intimate tables of 6 in the Gangster Room to full restaurant buyouts for up to 70 guests. Every event is led by the culinary team of James Beard-nominated Chef Scott Crawford, with custom menus built around your preferences and vision for the night.

How many guests can you accommodate?

The Private Dining Room seats up to 24 for a seated dinner or cocktail reception. The Gangster Room seats 6 guests behind the glass wall of our dry-aging room. Full restaurant buyouts accommodate up to 70 guests with exclusive use of the dining room, bar, and all private spaces.

What does the menu look like?

We offer Family Style (3, 4, or 5 courses) or Plated (3 or 4 courses) options. Sample dishes include Fresh Oysters, Poached King Crab, Seared Diver Scallops, Prime Ribeye, Dry-Aged Bone-In Ribeye, Australian Lamb Chop, and Baked Alaska for dessert. We build custom menus for every event based on your preferences, dietary considerations, and the story you want to tell.

How far in advance should I book?

For the Private Dining Room and Gangster Room, 2 to 4 weeks is ideal. For full restaurant buyouts, 4 to 6 weeks. Holiday weekends and wedding season (May through October) fill up faster, so book earlier if possible during those times.

Where is Crawford Brothers located?

401 Fenton Gateway Dr., Cary, NC 27511 — inside Fenton, right on the Cary-Raleigh line. We are 12 minutes from downtown Raleigh, 15 minutes from North Hills, and 25 minutes from Durham. Free parking in the Fenton garage. For wedding parties staying at hotels in Raleigh or RTP, we are an easy drive with no downtown traffic to navigate.

How do I start planning?

Contact our events coordinator at bryan@crawfordbrotherssteakhouse.com or call (919) 629-4029. We will walk you through menu options, room configurations, beverage packages, and timing to make sure your rehearsal dinner is exactly what you envision.

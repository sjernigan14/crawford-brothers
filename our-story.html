The Crawford Brothers Story

Chef Scott Crawford opened Crawford Brothers in December 2024 with a singular vision: to create the most refined steakhouse experience in the Triangle. With a career spanning decades in high-end dining, Chef Scott had spent years perfecting his craft at some of the country's most respected restaurants. He had earned recognition from the James Beard Foundation five times over as a semifinalist for Best Chef: Southeast, and in 2025 he was nominated for Outstanding Restaurateur. But after years of leading other people's restaurants, he was ready to build something of his own.

The concept of Crawford Brothers began with an obsession: prime beef. Chef Scott spent months researching dry-aging techniques, supplier relationships, and the science of moisture and enzymatic breakdown. He wanted a restaurant where every element of the steak experience—from aging to seasoning to finishing—could be controlled with absolute precision. This wasn't just about serving expensive meat; it was about respecting the product and the craft.

A Prime Vision

Walking into Crawford Brothers, you immediately understand the restaurant's philosophy. The dry-aging room sits behind glass in the center of the dining room, a constant reminder of the foundation that everything else is built upon. This is not a steakhouse that happens to age beef. This is a temple to prime beef, and everything else—the cocktails, the seafood, the service—exists in perfect harmony around it.

The menu reflects Chef Crawford's commitment to sourcing the absolute best. Prime New York Strips and Bone-In Ribeyes arrive through selective relationships with premium suppliers, then age in-house for weeks, allowing natural enzymes to break down muscle fibers and concentrate flavor. Japanese A5 Wagyu, Snake River Farms beef, and Australian lamb chops round out the protein program. Every cut is chosen not just for its source, but for how it will perform at different cooking stages and weight levels.

Beyond the Steak

But Crawford Brothers is far more than a steakhouse in the traditional sense. Chef Scott's background in fine dining is evident in every aspect of the operation. The seafood program is built around a daily-changing Seafood Tower of raw oysters, shrimp, snow crab claws, tuna tartare, and king crab salad. Siberian caviar comes with traditional accompaniments. Fresh diver scallops and braised beef short ribs round out a menu that treats premium proteins with equal reverence, whether they swim or walk.

The appetizer program showcases technique and restraint. Roasted Bone Marrow, served traditionally with parsley and fleur de sel. Steak Tartare, ground and finished tableside. Duck Meatballs. Poached King Crab. Each dish is designed to elevate the dining experience without overshadowing the main event.

The dessert program is built around the restaurant's signature Baked Alaska, a tableside theatrical finale that brings the dining experience full circle. A steakhouse chocolate cake and ginger cheesecake round out a program that respects tradition while maintaining Chef Crawford's exacting standards.

The Cocktail & Wine Program

No steakhouse experience is complete without a thoughtfully curated beverage program, and Crawford Brothers takes this seriously. The cocktail program features original creations designed to complement the menu—from the signature Crawford Old Fashioned to Tableside Martini Service. The wine program is built around bottles that stand up to prime beef and fresh seafood, curated in partnership with expert sommeliers. In the private Gangster Room, sommelier-led wine pairings can be tailored to your multi-course meal.

The bar program extends beyond wine and cocktails. Amaro selections and fine spirits offer a sophisticated way to close out your evening, each choice made to round out the Crawford Brothers experience.

Private Dining & Events

Crawford Brothers offers three distinct private dining experiences. The Private Dining Room accommodates up to 24 guests in an elegant space within the main restaurant. The Gangster Room seats 6 guests directly behind the glass of the dry-aging room, with a direct view of prime cuts aging on the rack. And for larger celebrations, Full Restaurant Buyouts offer exclusive use of the entire space for up to 70 guests.

Whether you're planning a corporate dinner, rehearsal dinner, milestone celebration, or an intimate evening with friends, the team at Crawford Brothers—led by Chef Scott and his carefully assembled culinary staff—will create an event tailored specifically to your group. Multi-course family-style and plated dinners showcase dishes from the regular menu, customized for your party. Exclusive beverage experiences, from Tableside Old Fashioned to curated whiskey tastings, add another dimension to the celebration.

The Fenton Location

Crawford Brothers sits at 401 Fenton Gateway Dr. in downtown Cary, part of the Fenton development—a thoughtfully designed mixed-use space that honors the area's history while creating something entirely new. The restaurant's design echoes a classic steakhouse aesthetic, with warm woods, leather, and the dramatic centerpiece of the dry-aging room. The team deliberately chose a location that would serve as a destination, not just a neighborhood spot, befitting the level of care and attention to detail that goes into every service.

Open daily from 5pm to 10pm, Crawford Brothers welcomes diners year-round. Reservations through Tock ensure that the dining experience is never rushed, and that the kitchen can prepare each table with the same meticulous care that defines the restaurant's identity.

Looking Forward

In just its first months of operation, Crawford Brothers has earned recognition from the most respected names in food media and hospitality. Wine Enthusiast, Eater, Raleigh Magazine, and others have already celebrated the restaurant's craft and vision. But for Chef Scott Crawford and his team, the work is never finished. Every service is an opportunity to refine, to improve, to push the standards of excellence even higher.

Crawford Brothers is more than a steakhouse. It's the culmination of decades of culinary experience, brought together in service of a single ideal: to honor the finest ingredients with the finest technique, and to create an experience worthy of the trust our guests place in us every evening.

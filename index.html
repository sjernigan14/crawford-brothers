Crawford Brothers Steakhouse

Crawford Brothers is the vision of James Beard-recognized Chef Scott Crawford — a prime steakhouse built around one principle: the finest ingredients, prepared with precision, and served in a setting that feels both timeless and alive. Every steak on our menu is dry-aged in-house in our dedicated aging room.

Dry-Aged In House

The Steaks

We believe that a great steak begins long before it hits the plate. Our entire program is built on the philosophy that time, temperature, and expertise transform quality beef into something extraordinary. Every cut on our menu is meticulously selected and aged in our dedicated in-house facility, where we control every variable to achieve the perfect balance of tenderness, flavor, and depth. The result is steaks that showcase the true character of prime beef — rich, complex, and unforgettable.

Raw Bar & Seafood

Alongside our steaks, we've built a celebrated raw bar and seafood program. Working with the finest suppliers and purveyors, we bring in the best of the day — pristine oysters, diver scallops, and fish prepared with the same precision and care we apply to every dish that leaves our kitchen. Whether you're enjoying a dozen oysters or a perfectly seared piece of fish, you're tasting our commitment to sourcing only the best.

Cocktails & Wine

Our beverage program reflects our dedication to craft and excellence. Our cocktails are thoughtfully created by bartenders who understand balance and technique. Our wine list spans the globe, curated to complement our food and highlight the best producers and vintages. Whether you prefer a classic cocktail, a rare wine, or a thoughtful spirits selection, we've created a program that elevates every sip and every meal.

Private Dining

We offer three distinctive spaces for your private celebrations. The Private Dining Room accommodates up to 24 guests for intimate gatherings and executive dinners. The Gangster Room provides a sophisticated setting for 6 in our wine-surrounded private space. Or reserve us for a full buyout—up to 70 guests for weddings, corporate events, and celebrations of exceptional significance.

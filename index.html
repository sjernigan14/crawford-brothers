Crawford Brothers is the vision of James Beard-recognized Chef Scott Crawford — a prime steakhouse built around one principle: the finest ingredients, prepared with precision, and served in a setting that feels both timeless and alive. Every steak on our menu is dry-aged in-house in our dedicated aging room.

Dry-Aged In House

The Steaks

Prime cuts are aged in our dedicated dry aging room, developing the deep, concentrated flavor and buttery tenderness that only time and craft can produce. The Prime New York Strip, Dry-Aged Bone-In Ribeye, Japanese A5 Strip Loin, and Snake River Farms Eye of the Rib. Every cut served with house-made sauces.

Raw Bar & Seafood

Our Seafood Tower — oysters, shrimp, snow crab claws, tuna tartare, and king crab salad — is a shareable showpiece. Siberian caviar by the ounce. Seared Diver Scallops. Poached King Crab with Caviar Beurre Blanc. The menu rewards guests who explore beyond the steak section.

Cocktails & Wine

Our cocktail program features original craft cocktails and a signature Crawford Old Fashioned. The wine list is curated for pairing with prime beef and fresh seafood. After dinner, a rotating seasonal list of Amaro and fine after-dinner selections completes the evening.

Private Dining

We offer three distinct experiences. The Private Dining Room for up to 24 guests. The Gangster Room — an intimate 6-person table behind the glass of our dry aging room with a Butcher’s Feast for the Table. Full restaurant buyouts for up to 70 guests.

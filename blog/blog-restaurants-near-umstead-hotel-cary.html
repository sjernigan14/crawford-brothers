Prime Steakhouse Dining Near The Umstead

December 2025

The Steakhouse Your Umstead Stay Deserves

You're staying at The Umstead Hotel and Spa—the only Five-Star AAA Five Diamond hotel in the Triangle. The concierge recommended three restaurants on-property. They're competent. But you came to Raleigh for the prime steakhouse experience, and nine minutes away, that's exactly what you'll find at Crawford Brothers.

Located in the Fenton district just south of downtown, Crawford Brothers offers the kind of steakhouse dinner that pairs naturally with your Umstead stay. No resort pricing. No tired hospitality-hotel formality. Just Chef Scott Crawford's kitchen, dry-aged prime beef visible through glass, and a wine list that rewards serious attention.

What Sets Crawford Brothers Apart

The in-house dry-aging program is the centerpiece. Unlike most steakhouses that outsource aging and receive cuts prepped and wrapped, Crawford Brothers ages whole primals on-site. Glass walls run the length of the dining room—you can see the carcasses aging, understand the craft, and watch the kitchen work.

Chef Scott Crawford is a James Beard semifinalist. His culinary ambition doesn't manifest in needless complexity—it appears in a perfectly seared scallop, a properly rendered prime steak, and the discipline to let premium ingredients define the plate.

The seafood tower arrives regularly, stocked with oysters, shrimp, and crab. The wine program is overseen by Head Sommelier Mat Brochard, and the 300+ bottle list reflects genuine curation—not just depth, but thoughtfulness about what pairs with the menu.

The Practical Details

Location: 401 Fenton Gateway Dr., Cary, NC 27511—approximately 9 minutes from The Umstead depending on traffic.

Hours: Open daily, 5pm–10pm. Dinner-only. Plan accordingly if you're considering an earlier seating.

Reservations: Book through Tock. During peak season and weekends, tables fill early.

Private dining: Two options. The Gangster Room seats 6 and looks directly into the dry-aging glass—intimate, with theater. The Private Dining Room accommodates up to 24 and works for business groups or larger celebrations.

For Your Umstead Concierge

If you want to arrange a car, mention Crawford Brothers by name. It's a destination dinner, not an afterthought, and it deserves a proper arrival. The hotel concierge should be familiar—high-end guests frequently ask for alternatives to on-property dining, and Crawford Brothers is the natural answer for steakhouse seekers.

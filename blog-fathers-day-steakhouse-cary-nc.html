Father's Day at Crawford Brothers

Prime Steaks & Bourbon in Cary

Published: September 2025 Author: Crawford Brothers Steakhouse

Father's Day calls for a real steakhouse — not a chain, not a compromise. Crawford Brothers in Fenton, Cary delivers exactly that. Prime dry-aged cuts under glass, bourbon that reads like a manifesto, and the kind of room that lets a father breathe. This is where to land.

The Steaks: Dry-Aged and Visible

Walk in and you see it immediately — our dry-aging program behind glass walls. No theater. No pretense. Thirty-five to forty-five-day prime beef hanging in controlled conditions, darkening and concentrating. You can watch it happen. The steaks that come from that glass come aged exactly right — mineral, nutty, with a crust that takes a sear like it's supposed to.

For Father's Day, order a dry-aged ribeye or bone-in strip. Let him sit with it. The difference between this and supermarket beef exists because this beef has had time to become itself — less water, more flavor. That's not sentiment. That's chemistry.

The Bourbon Program

Beverage Director Jordan Joseph doesn't do volume. He does intention. The bourbon and whiskey program here is built on cocktails that matter — the CB Old Fashioned is the template, made with care and the kind of bourbon that deserves it. The Port of Spain follows close. Both are Father's Day staples.

The wine list runs over 300 bottles. That's not excess; that's range. Red Burgundy, California Cabernet, things that age. If your father knows wine like he knows steak, he finds it here.

The Gangster Room

Six seats. Behind the dry-aging glass. The Gangster Room is intimate — the kind of space where a Father's Day dinner becomes something he remembers. You can request it for your reservation. It fits the family that wants to keep it close.

The rest of the dining room runs open and airy — high ceilings, visible kitchen, bar sightlines. Crawford Brothers doesn't hide what it does.

Location and Reservations

We're in Fenton, at 401 Fenton Gateway Dr., Cary — the kind of location that's become central. Open daily 5pm to 10pm. Father's Day will be our busiest service; reserve ahead through Tock or call (919) 629-4029.

Why Crawford Brothers for Father's Day

A real steakhouse means prime beef aged properly, bourbon poured with knowledge, and a dining room that respects what happens at the table. Crawford Brothers does all three. No shortcuts. No compromise. That's the brand. That's why a father lands here on his day.

The steaks are real. The drinks are real. The experience is real. That's what separates a restaurant from a destination — and Father's Day, at least once, deserves the destination.
